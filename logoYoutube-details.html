YouTuber Logo Design

YouTuber Logo Design

In this project, I created a modern and dynamic logo for a YouTuber, tailored to suit a digital content creator's brand. The design features bold, clean lines with an integrated play button and camera icon to symbolize video creation and engagement. Using a vibrant color palette of red, black, and white, the logo reflects creativity, energy, and professionalism. It is versatile and adaptable, making it perfect for use across platforms, including YouTube banners, profile pictures, and merchandise.
